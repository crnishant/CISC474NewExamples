Giving you the latest info on this pandemic

Team from the University of Delaware

This website pulls in the latest data from Johns Hopkins University regarding the coronavirus outbreak within the United States. Hover over each state in the map to get basic data regarding the virus in that state. You may also look at more state specific data by switching over to the 'State Data' tab. And finally, if you would like to see whether or not 'the curve has flattened', feel free to see the 'Graph' tab for the total picture of the coronavirus in the US. And, as always, stay safe!
